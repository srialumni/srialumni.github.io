2025 Annual Reunion: September 25th

September 25, 2025 | Flyer

SRI Alumni Association members are invited to attend the annual reunion in Menlo Park on Thursday September 25, 2025. It will be held in the International Building from 4:00 until 7:00 p.m. The program will include talks from SRI CEO David Parekh and from Patrick Lincoln, President of SRI's Information and Computing Sciences Division. You can count on sumptuous hors d'oeuvres, excellent drinks, delightful conversation, and plenty of door prizes.

This year the reunion is free of charge for one current alumni member and one guest. Your additional guests are welcome to attend at a charge of $25 each. As an added bonus, paying guests who are former SRI employees but aren't currently members of the association will have their entrance fee go toward one year of association membership.

Please RSVP for you and one guest (if applicable) by September 16. If you need to renew your alumni dues ($25) and/or are bringing more than one guest, please bring $25 to the reunion or mail the sign-up form and include your payment by September 16.
