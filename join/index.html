Your first year of membership in the SRI Alumni Association is FREE! Join the alumni association via online form and receive the newsletter, invitations to all alumni events, and additional benefits (listed below). Alternately, print and mail in the Enrollment Form (PDF).

Thereafter, renew your membership annually for $25 per year to support the association and continue your benefits.

Membership Benefits

The association was founded in 1996 to provide former staff members the opportunity to keep in touch with SRI and with colleagues, to support the institute in a variety of ways, and to help perpetuate SRI’s traditions and values. We have a few hundred members across the United States and the world. SRI Alumni Association members enjoy many activities and services:

  • Alumni Association Newsletter: Published and emailed three times a year and containing news about SRI programs and Alumni Association activities, information about individual members, and obituaries of former SRI staff members.
  • Membership Directory: A directory of alumni contact information shared with association members annually.
  • Mailing List and Linkedin: Alumni list & SRI Alumni Linkedin.com group announcing news and events.
  • Annual Reunion Meeting: A catered event at SRI that provides an opportunity for
    • Socializing with other Alumni Association members
    • Viewing the Alumni Hall of Fame Induction ceremony
    • Hearing a speaker describe an important SRI project or organizational development
  • Spring Fling: A catered picnic or visit to a Bay Area point of interest. Past trips have been to the Computer History Museum, SLAC, NASA-Ames, and the California Academy of Sciences.
  • SRI Archives: A database and physical repository documenting important areas of SRI’s history, including photographic archives.
