In August 2025, the SRI Alumni Association announced an election for the Offices of the SRI Alumni Association Chair, Secretary, and Treasurer, and an intention to file for nonprofit incorporation. Guiding documents were created, and nominations were solicited in the August 2025 newsletter and on this website. Nominations were accepted through September 4, 2025.

No nominations were received from Association members. Nominations were received by steering committee members. One Treasurer was nominated and accepted. One of the nominees for Secretary tentatively accepted. No nominees for President accepted. At the September 11, 2025 steering committee meeting, committee members decided to pause incorporation when the committee became aware of new information about Association needs, incorporation costs, and the benefits of the Association remaining an official SRI organization. A majority of steering committee members agreed that the benefits of remaining an official SRI organization outweighed the benefits of incorporating as a separate nonprofit.

As an SRI organization (rather than a separate incorporated nonprofit), a vote for officers by the steering committee is sufficient. So an election by membership was not held. Officers nominated for Treasurer and Secretary accepted. The role of President/Chair remains open. The committee expects to discuss the possibility of Chair becoming a rotating position among steering committee members.

Please let us know if you have questions, concerns or suggestions! Send any questions or comments to: steering@srialumni.org
