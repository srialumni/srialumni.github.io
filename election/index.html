The SRI Alumni Association solicted nominations for the Offices of the SRI Alumni Association Chair, Secretary, and Treasurer through September 4, 2025. Nominations were solicited in the August 2025 newsletter and on this website. Nominations are now closed.

No nominations were received from Association members. However, 11 nominations were received by current steering committee members: 5 people for Chair, 5 people for Secretary, and 1 (multiple times) for Treasurer. The Treasurer has accepted. All but 3 remaining nominees have indicated that they will not to accept. The remaining 3 are now considering whether or not to accept. The committee expects that there will be at most one candidate accepting nomination for each of the remaining positions. If so, an election is not required (no contest). The committee will discuss and finalize candidate status by September 11, 2025. If the status changes and an election is required, and election will be called and an announcement sent to the membership email list. Otherwise, decisions and results will be announced at the Reunion.

Please let us know if you have questions, concerns or suggestions! Send any questions or comments to: steering@srialumni.org
